A Curious Girl

Zara was a cheerful girl who loved mysteries. She lived in a colorful little town where everyone seemed busy and happy.

One morning, Zara noticed something strange.

Old Grandma Noor's garden, which had been empty yesterday, was suddenly full of beautiful flowers.

"Who planted these?" Zara wondered.

Grandma Noor smiled. "I don't know. They were here when I woke up."

Zara's eyes sparkled. "A mystery!"
